We are constantly growing and adapting to the changes of society, and invite you to learn more about how you can make an impact.

Acknowledgments for Our Donors

As a token of our appreciation for your generous support, we have implemented a system of personalized digital certificates for those who contribute to our cause. After making your donation, we kindly ask you to send the corresponding receipt to our email address at donations@juntoscontralapobreza.org. Please include your full name in the email so that we can personalize your acknowledgment. Once received, our team will promptly design and send you your digital certificate. We deeply value your generosity, as it enables us to continue working toward our goals. Thank you for being a part of this mission!
